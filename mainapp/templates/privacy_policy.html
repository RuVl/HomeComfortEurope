3. Technical Information

We use the non-personally identifiable information and certain technical information about your computer in order to operate, maintain and manage heritagefurnitureuk.com

3.1 Cookies

Heritage Furniture UK Ltd uses cookies to help speed up the login process and for security purposes. A cookie is a small amount of data that identifies you as a unique user that is sent to your web browser from a web server and stored on your computer. We use cookies to improve the quality of our service and make the service easier to use for our customers. We do this by storing user preferences in cookies and by tracking user trends - for example, we use cookies to allow you to automatically log in to the service after the first user session.

Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies. You can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. Be aware, however, that some parts of www.heritagefurnitureuk.com may not function properly if you refuse cookies.

More detailed information on cookies may be found in the Help section of your web browser.

Heritage Furniture UK Ltd also provides analyses of our customers in the aggregate to prospective partners, advertisers and other third parties. Linked websites are not under the control of Heritage Furniture UK Ltd and we are not responsible for the conduct of companies linked to our website. Before disclosing any information on any other website we advise you to examine their terms and conditions of use.

5. Controlling Your Personal Information

5.1 Transfer of information

We will not supply your data to a third party for commercial exploitation unless explicitly authorised by you; so you can rest assured that we will not sell your name, personal details or email addresses to any third party without express consent from you.

The only circumstances in which we will provide any of your personal data to a third party for non-commercial purposes are those contained in this Privacy Policy.

6. Security And Prevention Against Loss, Misuse Or Alteration Of Information

6.1 Information storage

All information you provide to us is stored in the UK only on secure servers that are protected in secure facilities including firewall protection.

Heritage Furniture UK Ltd has implemented such technology and security features, as well as strict internal guidelines, to safeguard the privacy of your information from unauthorised access or improper use. We will continue to enhance security as new technology becomes available.

While we make every effort to ensure that your information is secure on our system, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, we cannot guarantee the security of any information you transmit to us, and you do so at your own risk.

Your Heritage Furniture UK Ltd website account information is password-protected for your privacy and security. You can play an important role in keeping your personal information secure by maintaining the confidentiality of your password used on the Heritage Furniture UK Ltd web site. We recommend that you change your password from time to time for additional security. Please advise us immediately if there is any unauthorised use of your account by any other Internet user or any other breach of security.

Internally, access to our customer database is only granted to key Heritage Furniture UK Ltd staff and the companies with whom we have hardware and software maintenance contracts (who are in turn bound by a privacy clause). In addition, the database is password protected to prevent unauthorised access. Backups are run to prevent loss of information, and our Internet servers are housed in secure facilities.

6.2 Data Retention Period

We will retain your personal data only as long as is necessary for the purposes to which you consent under Heritage Furniture UK Ltd Terms and Conditions and this Privacy Policy, or as is required by applicable law, and then we will delete it

6.3 Place Of Processing

The personal information held by us is held on and processed by computers situated in the United Kingdom
